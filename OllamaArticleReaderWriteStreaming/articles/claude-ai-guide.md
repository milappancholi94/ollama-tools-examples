# An Overview of the Claude AI Family

# Claude: Anthropic's Advanced AI Assistant

Claude is a family of large language models developed by Anthropic, known for their focus on safety and helpfulness in AI development. This family of models represents a significant step forward in natural language understanding and generation, aiming to create AI systems that are not only intelligent but also reliable and harmless.

The current range of Claude models includes highly capable versions such as Claude Fable 5, Opus 4.8, Sonnet 5, and Haiku 4.5. These models demonstrate strong performance across a wide array of complex tasks, excelling particularly in areas like coding, handling long agentic tasks, detailed writing, and deep analysis.

One of Claude's most distinguishing features is its ability to manage vast amounts of information through an exceptionally large context window, with recent models supporting up to 1 million tokens. Furthermore, the models exhibit adaptive thinking, allowing them to intelligently decide how much reasoning is necessary for a given task, enhancing efficiency and accuracy. Claude also features advanced capabilities for tool use and function calling, enabling it to take actionable steps by interacting with external tools.

Claude is accessible through various channels, including its official API, the claude.ai web application, and specialized tools like the Claude Code CLI. Anthropic's commitment to safety guides the development of these models, positioning Claude as a powerful and responsible tool for developers and users alike.
